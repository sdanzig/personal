Well, as mentioned in my description, poetry is one of my hobbies. I don't write poetry for the purpose of merely expressing myself.  In fact, I believe that simply saying bluntly what you mean is more productive than writing a poem to express yourself.  I write poetry for the challenge of it.  To follow such strict guidelines and pull out the perfect words to expand upon the imagery one tries to create in poetry is an immaculate art form.  I decided to put my recent poems up for viewing on the web. Almost all of them are sonnets, which are fourteen line poems with ten syllables per line. They can follow either English or Italian rhyming schemes, but often enough the patterns are changed arbitrarily. Anyway, there are a few sonnets that are written for rituals I perform in live-action roleplaying, where I am a cleric of Luminus, the goddess of life.
