Holy Ground Ritual

With peace in our lives and love in her light,
We trod throughout the troubled trails to try,
To strengthen the goodwill of her gift, life,
To set the path of endless death awry.
May her loved divinity grace this ground,
May her magic thrive from her precious earth,
May her coveted shine embrace, around
This slice of the world she gave life and mirth.
Let the sky's majesty see far below,
Her endless kindness, which we strive to show,
Empowering our magic with her serene soul,
With her sacred blessings, our magic flows.
Her holiness and light is now unbound,
All need not fear, for this is holy ground!
