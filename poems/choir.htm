The Choir

Wooed to an awe by the wind in the air,
Caressed by a charm and a beaming light,
Such a pleasing psalm and gift to the ear.
Constraints of joy vanish, voices take flight.
Melodic muses, harmonic heaven,
Gorgeous, graceful, flowing, flattering song.
Precious permeations, bless'd when given,
Soul lifted to heart, then smiles along.
The soft, swift soaring of a fantasy,
And fantastic flaunting of floral words.
The playful pushing as thoughts go racing,
Plagued not by a flaw as voices converge.
Sing me to stars, O angels of the dawn,
Till dusk do I dream of beautiful song.
