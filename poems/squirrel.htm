Ode to the Squirrels

Scuffling about, curious little squirelees,
You scamper about, burrowing under trees.
Furry, you scrape dirt in a hurry.
You scurry, you pounce in dance happily.
You're no tender pretender, my squirrelees,
Of rugged sprung foot. Scavenge for munchees!
Dexterous, agile squirrelees, so playfully
Dodging feet and contraption, you sillies.
Stampede approaching, run to the bushees!
You cower and wait--then do as you please.
O adorable squirrelees, so cute to see,
Careless and shameless, as we trip o'er thee.
Fun lil' rodents, fun lil' runts, keep busy
And bouncy, my fun-loving lil' squirrelees.
