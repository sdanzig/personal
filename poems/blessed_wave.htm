Blessed Wave

Sleeping windows under summer's windflow,
Taillights flare and glare before a linkless
train which snakes, no breaks, an endless sequence,
Fangs enraged beneath a flaming afro!

Spirit lost, a blacktop purgatory,
Shadowed eyes with glints of laughter,
Asshole makes my drive a huge disaster,
Any longer be extremely gory!

Billowed steam as fists go tight and knuckles
white and grating teeth of thund'ring anger,
Wait for inf'nite wanker after wanker,
Pedal floored when traffic fin'ly buckles.

Sleeping windows under summer's windflow,
Taillights flare and glare before a linkless
train which snakes, no breaks, an endless sequence,
Snake is sliced and hand is waved -- my hero.

Spirit lost, a blacktop purgatory,
Eyes of mercy, softened, show compassion,
With resolve to stop--so daring, dashing,
Stopping seemed to be obligatory.

Darkened clouds divide for streaming sunshine,
Such serene a smile past the stop sign.
