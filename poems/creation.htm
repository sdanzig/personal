Creation

Desolate day of redundant drivel,
Vast but void of point and pride,
Flashes of fire in the dreary drought,
Kindles cogitative conception.

Contemplating the contemplation,
Imagining its impeccable implementation,
Contriving colorful composition and configuration,
Playful permutation of perspective proliferating prediction.

Elevating to exhilarating excitation enthusiastically,
Accumulating adrenaline anticipating with artistic anxiety,
Vocalizing vivacious vagaries with lunatic loquacity,
Fantasizing of tantalizing fruition with freehearted felicity.

Focus, concentrate on complications,
Intense inspiration invigorates the impetus of improvisation and improvement,
Persevere, overcome the obstacles,
Rallying, racing, rushing to reach the realization’s resplendent resolution!

Alleviation,
Elation,
Pride,
Peace.
