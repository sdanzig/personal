Resurrection Ritual

Lustrous light from the love of Luminus,
Send this righteous spirit back safe and sound,
Save it from the death in which it is bound,
Bring life its breath, and let life bloom in us.
May this sad spirit have a fate foretold,
Basking merrily in the joys of life.
May this sore spirit avoid further strife,
And be granted the life it yearns to hold.
Vigorous and invaluable light,
Let pleasure relieve the pain partaken,
Let death be destroyed in this holy rite,
Soul's essence saved for the soul forsaken.
Your afterlife, I have redirected,
Your route is changed, you are resurrected!
