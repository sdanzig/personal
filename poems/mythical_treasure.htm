Mythical Treasure

Golden glimmers graze good-spirited greed,
Grappling with glamour, going for glory,
A seductive, sacrilegious story,
Of stouthearted souls that seldom succeed.
Permutations of possibilities,
And potentials, and precipitations,
Race reprehensible reservations,
And risks outrun responsibilities.
Much magnetism made immobilized,
Fixed on a fascinating fantasy,
Which forbids fingers and ensnares only eyes,
Mixed romantic angst for a man t’see.
And truths of the treasure’s trials emerge,
When reality’s lost and hearts converge.
