Inamorata

Flowing hair evokes the golden moonlight,
Dancing calmly over windswept water,
Soaring over wheat fields in the country,
Softly curved with waves of satin ardor.

Her smile shines with warmth and tenderness,
Her lips in such divinely sculpted form,
Resembling petals of an orchid's bloom,
And gentle sands upon a desert's dune.

Quite an angel's silhouette reflected
By a mirror framed in all her glory,
With the sinewed grace of heaven, whether
Slack or sweeping like an angel's feather.

As she expresses fantasy and fear,
Soigné and silken words course through the air.
Beneath her golden-hearted glitt'ring gaze,
There lies a heart endearing autumn days.
