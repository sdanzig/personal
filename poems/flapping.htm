Flapping

Flapping, fluttering, ferocious high flag,
The wind you carry, the wind you divide,
Endlessly, relentlessly--till you sag.
I fear the power you amass and hide.
There's no end to wind, no end to your beat.
'Loft in the sky, can be soft, but not shy.
Your pride? Could pride sate your heart and your heat?
O valiant flag, do not flitter me by!
To hold and to harness breath of the clouds,
To wrap, flap, and fold the gift that they share.
A spurious volley forms mounds you ripple 'round.
No breeze can compare, to one shift'd made fair.
I envy your lust, the longing within,
And the accomplished joy I crave... I can't win.
