Holy Weapon Ritual

Glorious power, my goddess shall grant,
To service life, the laud of Luminus,
To help vanquish all shadows within us.
May it destroy evil where life be scant,
And shine valiantly in the wake of dead.
O Love of Luminus, let Noctus feed,
As slain heartless, venemous lives shall lead.
A crusade of light over darkness tread.
Fierce beacon of death, may justice be served,
May joy be granted, and jolliness flood,
Let life be strengthened, when a life has swerved,
From her lips and breasts, to her lake of blood.
Luminus, bless this arm till the morrow,
By the power of life, set this aglow!
