Brooklyn




Why do I have Brooklyn listed in my Ireland-France vacation photos?  I met up with my Belarusian-American friend Aliona at Brighton Beach in Brooklyn (she likes places that start with B) and had some authentic Russian cuisine with her.  Here she is eating borscht.  We drank a lot of vodka afterward.  I had been preparing for my upcoming Guinness-fest, so my alcohol tolerance was pretty good.  I thought I kept pace with her, but she probably was being nice to me.



After dinner, we hung out on the beach for a while before I headed off to the airport.  Here's a picture of a seagull.
