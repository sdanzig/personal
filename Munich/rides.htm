Rides

 

Rides around Oktoberfest were colorful, fast, n' festive.  You see here a rollercoaster in the background.  It's no Space Mountain, sure, but people don't quite go to Oktoberfest for the rides...or maybe they do... I don't know how many amusement parks Germany has.

 

No fair is complete without a Ferris wheel.  I like the cloudy sky in the background.

 

We saw this guy going around and around on his little contraption for what seemed like all day.  Something tells me he didn't drink much beer.  Nevertheless, Bavarians have interesting hobbies.
