Shoeshine Drama








Jessica, wearing her favorite rabbit hat, diligently shines Aliona's boots.







Jessica proudly finishes her work then requests payment.







Aliona kindly pays with a tangerine. Jessica is a bit put off.







Jessica examines the tangerine, looking for any redeeming qualities.







Jessica politely declines the tangerine and requests cash.







Jessica returns the tangerine.







Aliona escapes to the subway, but Jessica follows, and again Aliona offers the tangerine.







Jessica refuses but Aliona insists.







Jessica finally eats the tangerine and shows Aliona the peel.







Jessica angrily tries to smash the tangerine peel into Aliona's face. Aliona blocks.







Aliona suggests to Jessica that she was foolish not to accept her tangerine as payment, and now she will die.







The feisty rabbit head launches her ear at Aliona's face, but Aliona again blocks.







Aliona, having had enough of this outburst, chops at Jessica's brain.







Jessica deftly thwarts this attack...







...and then attempts to poke Aliona's eyes out. With a quick chop, Aliona blocks this retaliation.







Jessica, having realized she has met her match, offers her most prized possession, her rabbit hat, to Aliona.







Aliona, quite the fashionista, is ecstatic to be given this fabulous gift.







NOT! Jessica retracts the offer at the last second, and Aliona is sad.
