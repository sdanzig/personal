Lord Pinkerton Gdansk

 

"Lord Pinkerton" I can't explain, but "Gdansk" is the Polish name for the city "Danzig", which has quite a history behind it, and is where my family name came from.  This trooper lived 15½ years, dying the night I got back from my college graduation ceremony at Rutgers.  I wonder sometimes whether this dog hung on long enough to see that I made it through college.  By the way, from all the dogs I've seen, westies are among the best out there.  He's a harmless little dog, but he's not a wimp.  These dogs were trained to hunt down and kill foxes.  They're sturdy, and can play tug-of-war surprisingly well.  You can lift a stuffed animal a westie is biting into high in the air and the little dog will dangle there for a good minute or two.  Pinky even racked up a few kills, including my own parakeet, due to an unsightly accident.
