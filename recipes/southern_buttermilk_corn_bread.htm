Southern Buttermilk Corn Bread

  • 1½ c cornmeal
  • ½ c all-purpose flour
  • 1½ c buttermilk (at the grocery store, you can buy a powder mix version of buttermilk which is very convenient if you don't use buttermilk much)
  • ¼ c vegetable shortening plus a bit more to grease the pan
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • 2 large eggs

Heat oven to 450 F. Grease bottom and sides of an 8 or 9 inch square baking pan with vegetable shortening.

Mix all ingredients. Beat vigorously for 40 seconds. Pour into pan.

Bake for 25 to 30 minutes, or until the edges start to turn dark brown. Best served warm.
