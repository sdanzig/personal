Blueberry Crunch Cake

  • ½ c yellow cornmeal
  • ¼ c + 2 tbsp all purpose flour
  • ¼ c + 2 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ½ c buttermilk
  • ¼ c eggs
  • 1 tbsp canola oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1½ c fresh blueberries
  • ⅛ tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp powdered sugar

Preheat oven to 425 F and grease an 8 inch round baking pan.

In medium bowl, combine cornmeal, flour, ¼ cup of the granulated sugar, the baking powder and salt. Add buttermilk, egg, oil, and vanilla and stir just until combined. Pour into prepared pan. Top with berries. In a small bowl, combine the remaining 2 tablespoons granulated sugar and the cinnamon; sprinkle evenly over berries. Bake until golden (about 25 minutes). Let cool for 10 minutes; sprinkle powdered sugar over the cake and serve warm.
