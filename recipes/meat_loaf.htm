Meat Loaf

  • ½ c crushed saltine crackers
  • ½ c milk
  • 2 lb mixed ground meats: ⅔ lb beef, ⅔ lb veal, and ⅔ lb pork
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • ½ c freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • ¼ c minced fresh parsley leaves
  • ½ tsp minced garlic
  • 1 small onion, minced
  • 1 small carrot, peeled and minced
  • 1 tsp minced fresh sage leaves
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to personal taste
  • 3 slices bacon

Preheat the oven to 350 F. Soak the saltine cracker crumbs in the milk until the milk is absorbed (about 5 minutes).

Mix together all the ingredients except the bacon. Shape the meat into a loaf in a baking pan; top with bacon. Bake 45 to 60 minutes, basting occasionally with rendered pan juices. When done, meat loaf will be lightly browned and firm, and a meat thermometer inserted into the center of the loaf reads 160 F.
