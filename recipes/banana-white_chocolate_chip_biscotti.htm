Banana-White Chocolate Chip Biscotti

  • 1 c sugar
  • ½ c butter (1 stick)
  • ½ c mashed very ripe banana (one medium banana, on the larger side)
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 c all-purpose flour
  • 3 tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ½ c white chocolate chunks (dice up white chocolate baking bar into ½ inch cubes)

Heat oven to 350 F.

Beat sugar and butter in large bowl with electric mixer on medium speed, or mix with spoon. Beat in banana, vanilla and eggs until smooth. In separate bowl, mix the flour, baking powder and salt thoroughly, then combine with the banana-egg mixture. Stir in chocolate.

Divide dough in half. Shape each half into 10 x 3 inch rectangle on cookie sheet with greased hands (dough will be sticky).

Bake about 25 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean and no longer. Don't let the edges get more than slightly golden-brown. Cool on cookie sheet 15 minutes.

Cut crosswise into ½-inch slices. Turn slices cut sides down on cookie sheet. Bake until it just starts to brown on top (approximately 10 minutes). Flip cookies over. Bake about 10-15 minutes longer or until golden brown. Remove from cookie sheet to wire rack to cool.
